# Privacy

Tally runs entirely on your device.

- Your transactions, categories, accounts, and settings are stored locally in a SQLite database on your phone. They are never sent to any server we operate.
- No accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no tracking, no ads.
- The app makes no network calls except the standard App Store / Play Store review prompt.

## Optional features that change where data lives

These are off by default. Each is opt-in, and the data still does not pass through any server we operate.

- **iCloud / Google Drive sync.** If you turn this on in Settings, Tally writes a copy of your data to your own iCloud Documents (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) so it can sync across your devices on the same Apple ID or Google account. We never see this file. You can turn it off, and you can delete the file from your cloud storage at any time.
- **Receipt scan.** On supported devices, Tally can read receipt photos using the on-device AI built into your phone (Apple Intelligence on iOS, Gemini Nano on Android). Photos are processed locally and discarded — they are never saved, never uploaded, and never sent to any server.
- **Platform-level backups.** If you have iCloud Backup or Android Auto Backup enabled at the OS level, your phone may include Tally's local data in those backups. That's a setting you control with Apple or Google, not with us.

If any of this ever stops being true, this file will say so.
